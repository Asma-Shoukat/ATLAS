BACKGROUND | Context & Multi-Turn RAG Gaps | The Dialogue History Challenge | ▪ | Standard RAG Limit: | Traditional systems treat each user query as a standalone statement. In natural conversations, questions are incomplete and context-dependent. | ▪ | Coreference Pronoun Drift: | Queries like 'what are its goals?' fail in vector search because pronouns cannot be mapped back to their original subjects. | ▪ | Naïve Stacking Flaw: | Simply appending history increases query noise and causes vector drift towards older turns, degrading search accuracy. | The Hallucination & Sufficiency Gap | ▪ | Speculation Hazard: | LLMs confidently fabricate details and answers when the retrieval engine delivers irrelevant or insufficient context. | ▪ | Abstention Deficit: | Standard systems lack a reliable safety gate to recognize evidence gaps, preventing honest 'I don't know' responses. | ▪ | Black-Box Opacity: | Generative responses lack sentence highlights or citation trace logs, making outputs difficult to audit by operators.
